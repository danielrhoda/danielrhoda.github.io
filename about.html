About

Hi! I'm Danny Rhoda, an evolutionary biologist ....

I'm currently in my second year of graduate school in the University of Chicago's Committee on Evolutionary Biology, working with Ken Angielczyk. My dissertation will focus on the jaws of macrostomatan snakes, employing adaptive landscapes and evolutionary quantitative genetic techniques to disentangle the processes influencing their extraordinary diversification.
